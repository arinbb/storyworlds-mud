A GoMUD-based MOO where players explore fictional worlds from movies, books, and artwork.

**Core concept:**
- Home zone is a "Library" with shelves of books, movies, and art
- Players pick up media objects (books, DVDs, paintings) and interact with them (open book, load into media player, step into painting)
- Interaction transports them to that fictional world as its own GoMUD zone
- Each zone is an homage to the complexity of its source material

**Design priorities:**
- Exploration and atmosphere over combat
- Rich descriptions, idle messages, and NPC interactions that capture the feel of each fictional world
- Battles exist but are secondary to representing literary/cinematic/artistic scenes
- Each fictional world uses a consistent zone framework for reuse

**Why:** The user wants to build this as their own GoMUD instance. They want dynamic, reusable patterns for creating scenes, objects, NPCs, and interactive elements across many fictional world zones.

**How to apply:** Every design decision should prioritize immersion, atmosphere, and faithful representation of source material over game mechanics.
